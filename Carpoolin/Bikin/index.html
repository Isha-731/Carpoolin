Why choose Carpoolin?

Carpoolin offers a bunch of advantages, both for you and the Environment.

About us

Financial Savings

Sharing the ride means splitting gas, tolls, and parking fees. This can add up to significant savings, especially with rising fuel costs.

Reduced Traffic Congestion

With fewer cars on the road, traffic jams become less frequent, saving everyone time. Some areas even have High Occupancy Vehicle (HOV) lanes that allow carpools to bypass traffic jams.

Social connections

Carpooling can be a great way to meet new people, build friendships with colleagues, or simply have some company on your ride.

Environmental benefits

By taking fewer cars off the road, carpool makes us to reduce air pollution and greenhouse gas emissions. This contributes to a cleaner, healthier environment.

Features

Carpoolin facilitates Carpooling in a few different ways, with some features depending on the method.

Multi-modality.

Integrating with public transport, our platform allows users to combine carpooling with other forms of transportation.

  • Two-Wheelers (Bikes)
  • Three-Wheelers (Autos)
  • Four-Wheelers (Cars)

Real-Time Information

Real-time information is crucial for a smooth and efficient carpooling experience.

  • Live location tracking.
  • Traffic updates.
  • Estimated Time of Arrival.

Group Travel

Travelling with the Same people on a Regular Basis in case of fixed Destination.

  • Travelling with nearby residential Collegues.
  • Travelling to home-town.
  • Travelling at Concerts, Stadiums, Museums.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I find carpool partners?

    Using Carpoolin, you will get notified about the people going through the same route as you, hence you will get to know about the carpool partners.

  • How do we decide on costs and pick-up/drop-off locations?

    Our Carpoolin Platform, makes it easy for you by not worrying about passenger fares. Our automated systems efficiently works on the Calculations and displays the most accurate fare to each passenger.

  • Is carpooling safe?

    Carpoolin is ultimately safe, as it provides the Real-time Location Sharing with you near and dear ones, throughout your journey along with the vehicle and Driver details.
